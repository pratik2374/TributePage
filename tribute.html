Here's time line of Tony Stark:

  • 1970 - Born in Manhattan, NewYork from very famous businessman, genius inventor Howard Stark and a socialite and philanthropist Maria Stark
  • 1984 - He was a child prodigy, at the age of 14 he went to study in MIT for 2 years showcasing his intelligence
  • 1991 - When he was about in his mid 20's, his father and mother were on holiday in Bahamas, and planned to go Pentagon to deliver the super soldier serum but in the mid way were killed by winter soldier also known as 'buki' but in a captative state by Hydra
  • 1992-2010: Tony inherits Stark Industries, his family's multi-billion dollar conglomerate, and assumes the role of CEO. He rapidly expands the company's technological advancements, propelling it to the forefront of military and civilian technology. During this period, Tony lives a hedonistic lifestyle, enjoying the trappings of wealth and fame while honing his skills as a brilliant inventor.
  • 2008: Tony Stark's life takes a dramatic turn when he is captured by a terrorist group known as the Ten Rings while demonstrating Stark Industries' new "Jericho" missile in Afghanistan. Forced to build weapons, Tony instead creates the first Iron Man suit powered by a miniature arc reactor to escape captivity.
  • 2010: Tony publicly reveals his identity as Iron Man, challenging the traditional notion of masked superheroes. He faces off against Obadiah Stane, his father's former business partner, who becomes the villainous Iron Monger.
  • 2011: Tony joins forces with other superheroes to form the Avengers, a team assembled to combat extraterrestrial threats. They successfully repel an invasion led by Loki, Thor's adoptive brother.
  • 2013: Tony faces a personal crisis when he struggles with post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD) following the Battle of New York. He confronts the Mandarin, a terrorist mastermind who threatens his world, only to discover the Mandarin's true identity is a facade orchestrated by Aldrich Killian.
  • 2015: Tony's hubris leads to the creation of Ultron, a rogue artificial intelligence designed to protect Earth but ultimately seeks to annihilate humanity. The Avengers must confront Ultron and prevent global catastrophe.
  • 2016: The Avengers face division over the Sokovia Accords, government regulations aimed at controlling superheroes. Tony supports the accords, leading to a rift with Captain America and other allies.
  • 2018: Thanos, a powerful cosmic warlord, launches his quest to obtain the Infinity Stones and wipe out half of all life in the universe. Tony confronts Thanos and ultimately sacrifices himself by using the Infinity Stones to save humanity during the cataclysmic events of Infinity War.
  • 2023: Tony's legacy lives on as his protege, Peter Parker, takes up the mantle of Spider-Man, embodying the ideals of heroism and self-sacrifice that defined Tony's journey.
